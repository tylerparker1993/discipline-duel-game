## What It Does

The Discipline Duel makes the idea of self-discipline feel concrete by turning it into an interactive challenge. Rather than passively consuming advice about productivity, you step into a game where your own mindset becomes the arena. Each round throws a familiar excuse or mental roadblock at you, and you answer with a deliberate "Counter-Strike" to push through it. That format helps turn discipline tactics into something you can practice, not just read about.

The experience centers on two main measures: your Resilience HP, which stands for how well you can absorb setbacks, and your Future Focus Score, which reflects how strongly you prioritize long-term goals. As you move forward, the game introduces tougher situations that echo real-world procrastination triggers and temptations. It is built for replay value, so each session can reveal new patterns in how you think while also giving you practical responses you can carry into day-to-day decisions.

## FAQ

**How do I get started?**  
Open the game URL in your browser and press the start button. You do not need an account, login, or download.

**Is there a way to keep my progress?**  
Yes. Progress is stored in your browser's local storage, so clearing browser data will reset your scores.

**Can I write my own Counter-Strikes?**  
Yes. If you do not want to use the guided choices, you can enter custom responses.

**Will it run on phones?**  
Yes. The game is responsive and works in modern mobile browsers.

**How often do scenarios change?**  
The game ships with a built-in excuse library that gets randomized each session. Future updates may expand the content.
